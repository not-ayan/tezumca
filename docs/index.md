## Programme Overview

The MCA programme at Tezpur University is a 2-year postgraduate course consisting of four semesters, requiring a minimum of 82 credits. It builds a strong foundation in computer science, software development, and modern computing technologies.

### Key Details

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Duration** | 2 years (4 semesters) |
| **Total Credits** | 82 |
| **Core Courses** | 58 |
| **Electives** | 18 |
| **Open Electives** | 6 |

📄 **[View Full Curriculum](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1Di0SzGis8jkqFLI-PvGkYL-IMoug6y1C/view?usp=drive_link)**

---

::: info Important Note for Non-CS Students

If you're coming from a non-Computer Science background, we recommend reviewing **Data Structures** concepts before starting the programme. Additionally, take **Selected Topics in Computing (STC)** in your first semester.

:::

## Admission Process

The MCA programme at Tezpur University offers admission through **two pathways**:

### TUEE - Tezpur University Entrance Examination
A university-specific entrance exam covering aptitude, logical reasoning, mathematics, and computer fundamentals.
