# Admission

A step-by-step guide covering the MCA admission process at Tezpur University — from receiving your rank to completing final enrollment.

## Overview

Once the TUEE / CUET results are published, eligible candidates are called for a **counselling and admission process**. This section covers everything you need to know after getting your rank.

## Admission Stages

1. **Result & Merit List** — Rank list is published on the official portal.
2. **Counselling Call Letter** — Shortlisted candidates receive a call letter via email/portal.
3. **Document Verification** — Original documents are verified on the counselling day.
4. **Seat Allotment** — Seats are allotted based on rank, category, and availability.
5. **Fee Payment** — Admission fee is paid online to confirm the seat.
6. **Enrollment** — Final enrollment and joining formalities at the university.
