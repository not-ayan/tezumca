# Fee Structure

Details about the MCA program fees at Tezpur University.

## Fee Breakdown

| Fee Component        | Amount (Approx.)                          | When Payable                  |
|----------------------|-------------------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Online Admission Fee | ₹19,000                                   | At time of online admission   |
| Hostel Admission Fee | ₹6,500 (₹5,825 for SC/ST candidates)     | During document verification  |
| Mess Fee             | ₹2,200 – ₹2,800                           | During hostel admission       |

> ⚠️ Fee amounts are approximate and subject to change each academic year. Always refer to the official fee notification for exact figures.
