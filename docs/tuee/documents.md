# Required Documents

A comprehensive guide to the documents you need to prepare and upload during the TUEE registration process.

## 1. Photo and Signature
You need to upload clear, scanned copies of your recent passport-size photograph and your official signature. 
💡 **Tip:** If you need to easily crop, compress, or resize your images to meet the portal requirements, you can use [Tinywow Image tools](https://tinywow.com/tools/image/) for free.

## 2. Income Certificate  

If applicable, your Income Certificate must be officially obtained from your local **Circle Office**.  

- Income certificate is **mandatory** for **SC, ST, OBC-NCL, and General-EWS** candidates.  
- If you do not have the certificate at the time of registration, you may submit an **undertaking** temporarily.  

### Important Notes  
- **General category** candidates do **not** need to submit an income certificate at the time of admission (document verification); the undertaking will be sufficient.  
- **SC, ST, OBC-NCL, and EWS candidates must submit the valid income certificate during admission/document verification.**

### Fee Waiver Information  
- There is **no fee waiver scheme at the time of admission**.  
- However, you can apply for a **Fee Waiver Scheme later**.  
- The scheme is available for **BPL students** under certain conditions, such as:  
  - Your **CGPA must be within the top 60% of the class**  
  - Other institute-specific terms and conditions may apply

## 3. Class X and XII Marksheets & Certificates
You will need your 10th and 12th board marksheets and certificates.
💡 **Tip:** If you need to edit these files or merge both the certificate and marksheet into a single document, you can use [TinyWow PDF Tools](https://tinywow.com/tools/pdf).

## 4. Graduation Results
You are required to upload your undergraduate marksheets based on your current status:
* **General Rule:** You need to create a securely merged **single PDF** containing all your previous semester results.
* **If 6th Sem Result is Awaited:** You might need to get an official clarification (certificate/letter) from your university stating that you have successfully passed in all the previous semesters.
* **If you have Backlogs:** If you have active backlogs and the updated result is still not declared, you must attach sequence-wise **all marksheets, including the backlog grade sheets**.

💡 **Tip:** Once you have all the individual sheets scanned, you can easily use [TinyWow PDF Tools](https://tinywow.com/tools/pdf) to create/merge them into a single PDF.

## 5. Caste Certificate
If applying under reserved categories (OBC-NCL, SC, ST, EWS), you must upload your caste/category certificate.
* The **Central Government format** is highly preferred for OBC-NCL & SC/ST, although exact explicit guidelines are not given on the portal. It is always safest to prepare the central format.
* For EWS both the **State Government format** & **Central Government Format** are accepted.
