# TUEE Registration

Details on how to register for the Tezpur University Entrance Examination (TUEE) for MCA.

## Important Dates
* 🗓️ Admission Portal Opens: 17 March 2026
* ⏳ Last Date to Apply: 30 April 2026
* 📝 TUEE Exam: First Week of June 2026

## Registration Link
* 🔗 [Register Here (Tezpur University Admission Portal)](https://www.tezu.ernet.in/adm26/)

## Eligibility Criteria
* Passed any graduation degree (e.g.: BE / BTech / BSc / BCom / BA / BVoc / BCA etc.) preferably with Mathematics at 10+2 level or at Graduation level.
* Obtained at least 50% marks in the qualifying examination.
