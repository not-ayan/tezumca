# Anti-Ragging Affidavit

📢 **Important Notice for TUEE & CUET Applicants**

All students applying for admission to the MCA programme at Tezpur University are **required** to submit an Anti-Ragging Affidavit during the registration and admission verification process.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official UGC Anti-Ragging portal: <br>
<a href="https://www.antiragging.in/affidavit_university_form.php" target="_blank" style="display: inline-block; padding: 6px 16px; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 8px; background-color: var(--vp-button-brand-bg); color: var(--vp-button-brand-text); border-radius: 20px; text-decoration: none; font-weight: 600; font-size: 14px;">Fill Anti-Ragging Affidavit Form</a>

2. Complete the form carefully using the detailed university information provided below.
3. Download the generated affidavit.
4. Keep it ready for submission during your document verification.

::: warning Mandatory Requirement
This document is strictly mandatory. Ensure you complete it in a timely manner.
:::

---

When filling out the form on the portal, use the exact details provided below to ensure your affidavit is correctly mapped to Tezpur University.
